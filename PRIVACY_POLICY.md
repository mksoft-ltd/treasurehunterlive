# Privacy Policy for Treasure Hunter Live

**Last Updated: November 24, 2025**

## Introduction

Treasure Hunter Live ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Treasure Hunter Live mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register and use our App, we may collect:
- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, and password
- **Profile Data**: User-created profile information, statistics, and achievements
- **User-Generated Content**: Photos, treasure descriptions, clues, and QR codes you create

### Location Information
- **Precise Location**: We collect your device's GPS coordinates to enable location-based treasure hunting features
- **Location History**: We store locations of treasures you create and find to provide gameplay functionality

### Camera and Photos
- **Camera Access**: We access your camera to:
  - Scan QR codes associated with treasures
  - Take photos of treasures you find
  - Create photo evidence for treasure claims
- **Photo Storage**: Photos you take are stored on our servers via Firebase Storage

### Device Information
- **Device Data**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, features used, session duration, and crash reports

### Payment Information
- **Subscription Data**: For premium subscriptions, we process payments through Google Play Billing
- **Payment details are handled securely by Google and not stored on our servers**

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the treasure hunting game functionality
- Process and verify treasure claims and discoveries
- Display your location on maps relative to nearby treasures
- Enable QR code scanning for treasure verification
- Process premium subscription purchases
- Display leaderboards and user statistics
- Send notifications about nearby treasures or game updates
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Firebase (Google LLC)
- **Firebase Authentication**: Manages user accounts
- **Cloud Firestore**: Stores user data, treasures, and game information
- **Firebase Storage**: Stores photos and images
- **Firebase Cloud Functions**: Processes backend logic
- **Firebase Cloud Messaging**: Sends push notifications
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Maps Platform
- **Google Maps SDK**: Displays maps and location data
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Play Billing
- **In-App Purchases**: Processes subscription payments
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure
- Communications are encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- User passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data:
- **With Other Users**: Your display name, profile photo, created treasures, and found treasures are visible to other players
- **With Service Providers**: Firebase and Google services as described above
- **For Legal Reasons**: If required by law or to protect our rights

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View your personal data through your profile
- **Update**: Edit your account information and profile
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable location services or notifications in device settings
- **Unsubscribe**: Cancel premium subscriptions through Google Play

To delete your account, contact us at info@froggyeye.com

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Location Data and Permissions

### Why We Need Location
- Find treasures near your current location
- Create treasures at specific locations
- Verify you are physically present at a treasure location to claim it
- Display your position on the map

### Location Control
You can control location access through your device settings. Disabling location will limit core gameplay features.

## Camera Permission

### Why We Need Camera Access
- Scan QR codes attached to physical treasures
- Take photos as proof of treasure discovery
- Create photo-based treasures

### Camera Control
You can revoke camera access in device settings. This will disable QR scanning and photo capture features.

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country. By using the App, you consent to such transfers. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:
- App notifications
- Email to your registered address
- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
